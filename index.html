04  ·  Sales Order — Channel Selection & Order Type
Selling Module
What is the order type?
Select at time of Sales Order creation
Pre-order SO
Pre-order Sales Order
Customer commits in advance — stock may not be ready yet. Fulfilled in multiple batches.
  • Future delivery date confirmed
  • Multiple partial delivery notes
  • Fulfilment 1 → Fulfilment 2 → … N
  • Stock reserved on availability
  • Balance qty tracked per SO line
Direct SO
Direct Sales Order
Stock is available now. Single fulfilment. Immediate pick-pack-dispatch cycle.
  • Immediate delivery commitment
  • Single delivery note
  • Stock reserved on SO submit
  • Pick list generated instantly
  • Invoice on delivery
